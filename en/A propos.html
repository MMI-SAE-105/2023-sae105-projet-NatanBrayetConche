Let me introduce myself: I'm Natan Brayet-Conche, a first-year MMI student at the Montbéliard IUT. This website is my very first project, carried out between September and January 2024 as part of my studies.

This project, supervised by Mr Malso, tackles a wide-ranging issue based on a film line. It deals with the film "I am Legend" through a guideline, the famous quote by Robert Neville: "My name is Robert Neville, I am a survivor, I live in New York".

The choice to explore "I am Legend" stems from my personal interest in the film, but also from its profound exploration of themes such as loneliness and human nature. The aim of this project is to immerse the user in the skin of an apocalypse survivor, for an immersive and introspective experience.
